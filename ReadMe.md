# Project motivation
Anomaly detection in stock market helps us identify unusual patterns to handle:
1. Risk Management
2. Fraud Detection and Prevention
3. Data Integrity and operational efficiency
4. Informed decision making
5. predicting "Black swan" events

Who benefits from this system? 
1. Financial institutions: banks, hedge funds, asset management firms
2. Traders and Investors
3. Regulatory bodies like SEC and FINRA
4. Cybersecurity teams

This Project doesn't:
1. predict future prices
2. give buy/sell advice
3. guarantee profit
